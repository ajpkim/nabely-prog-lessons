History of Ice Cream

Ice cream's origins are known to reach back as far as the second century B.C., although no specific date of origin nor inventor has been undisputably credited with its discovery. The ice cream we enjoy today is a relatively modern invention.

Flavors

Vanilla

Vanilla is often used as the default flavor in ice cream. It was first recorded as used in ice cream by Thomas Jefferson in 1780.

Chocolate

Chocolate was one of the first ice cream flavors created, and it has been a popular choice ever since.

Strawberry

Strawberry ice cream is made by blending in fresh strawberries into vanilla ice cream. It has been a beloved flavor for generations.
